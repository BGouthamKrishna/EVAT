About EVAT

EVAT is more than just a research project — it’s a vision to revolutionize how Australians engage with electric vehicles. Our AI-driven solutions enhance the charging experience through real-time availability forecasts, predictive demand planning, and accessibility insights. With our intuitive mobile application, navigating the EV landscape is smarter, faster, and more reliable than ever before.
