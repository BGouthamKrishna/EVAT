Electric Vehicle Adoption Tools (EVAT)

Empowering Australia's EV journey through AI, Machine Learning, and Smart App Development

Project Summary

The EVAT project is a comprehensive initiative aimed at accelerating electric vehicle adoption across Australia by bridging technology, infrastructure, and user-centric innovation. Through collaborative efforts between the Data Science and App Development teams, EVAT delivers predictive models, intelligent routing, blackspot detection, and an intuitive mobile application—all designed to make EV ownership smarter and more accessible.
